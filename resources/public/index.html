Amazon Chime SDK Firefox Tests

Scenario 1

Tile Errors

Steps

  1. Open this page on Chrome, and click on Start Meeting to start video
  2. Open this page on Firefox, and hit Start Meeting
  3. On Chrome, turn off and on several times your video stream with the toggle button.

Result: On Firefox, after Chrome video goes on and off several times, an error will be thrown and sometimes the tiles will freeze.

Scenario 2

Duplicated Tiles

Steps

  1. Open this page on two separate Chrome windows or tabs and start Video on both.
  2. Open this page on Firefox and start video

Result: There will be a duplicate tile from the Chrome attendees. Four, instead of three, tiles will be displayed on Firefox.
